## How this happened

Most genuine-dead entries are **fabricated `datasetUrl`s** — invented during the
bulk Feb-2026 expansion. The tell is the `github.com/SemEval/semeval-20XX-taskN`
pattern (no such org/repos) and `github.com/<MadeUpName>/...` shaped like a real
repo but 404. **Fix rule:** never swap in a guessed URL. Find the real link from
the paper's ACL Anthology page / project site / the dataset's HF page, confirm
HTTP 200 with a browser UA, then replace. If no canonical link exists, point
`datasetUrl` at the paper and drop the field rather than guess.
